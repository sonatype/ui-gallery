`.iq-threat-bar` is used at the left edge of a table cell or list item to indicate via colour the threat level of the information to follow. There are three scales to choose from: threat level by name, threat level by number, and policy threat level by number. The names and colour variables are located in the `/scss/_variables.scss` file. All three are represented in the table below. Other threat indicators such as threat indicators and threat backgrounds have similar purposes but different applications. Typically the threat level is set programmatically: `<span class="iq-threat-bar iq-threat-bar--critical"></span>` `<span class="iq-threat-bar iq-threat-bar--5"></span>` `<span class="iq-threat-bar iq-threat-bar--policy-level-2"></span>` For examples in the application look at: `/frontend/owner.manager/summary/owner.summary.view.html` `/frontend/owner.manager/summary/ownerPolicyList/ownerPolicyList.html`
